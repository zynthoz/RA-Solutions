#### Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment**: The site successfully avoids the worst SaaS AI slop. It feels grounded, industrial, and purpose-built for its audience. However, the four-column identical card grid for "Why RA Solutions" feels a bit too much like a template, and there are some nested card structures in the visual showcases that add unnecessary visual noise.

**Deterministic scan**: The detector found 23 issues across both pages. 
- **Low Contrast (18)**: Critical accessibility failures across both pages. The primary blue (`#3266e4`) fails against the dark background (4.0:1), and several light grey text elements inside the white cards fail against their backgrounds (e.g., `#ffffff` on `#10b981` green).
- **Skipped Headings (3)**: The outline jumps directly from `<h2>` to `<h4>` in multiple sections.
- **Nested Cards (1)**: The POS widget nests cards too deeply, creating visual clutter.
- **Typography (2)**: Use of 11px all-caps text for subtitles is too small for readability.

#### Overall Impression
The site does a fantastic job feeling like a real tool for real operators. The biggest missed opportunity right now is accessibility—the low contrast text and tiny font sizes actively hurt the "tired operators late at night" persona you're targeting.

#### What's Working
- **Industrial Tone**: The dark, high-contrast UI with glowing infrastructure lines perfectly nails the "built by engineers" vibe.
- **Show, Don't Tell**: The scroll-linked visual showcases of the POS and Report systems are highly effective at proving the product exists and works.
- **Copywriting**: The text is sharp, benefit-driven, and completely devoid of corporate fluff.

#### Priority Issues

- **[P0] Low Contrast Typography**
  - **Why it matters**: Laundromat operators reading this on their phones at 11 PM won't be able to read the primary blue accents or the grey subtitles.
  - **Fix**: Brighten `--primary` slightly for dark backgrounds, darken `--text-muted`, and change the green background on the payment card to a darker shade.
  - **Suggested command**: `polish`

- **[P1] Skipped Heading Levels**
  - **Why it matters**: Jumping from `<h2>` to `<h4>` breaks the document outline for screen readers and makes the structural hierarchy confusing.
  - **Fix**: Change the `<h4>` elements in the Features and Pillars sections to `<h3>`.
  - **Suggested command**: `audit`

- **[P2] Tiny All-Caps Text**
  - **Why it matters**: 11px uppercase text is very hard to read on mobile devices, especially for older users.
  - **Fix**: Increase the font size of `.mc-subtitle` to at least 12px (or `0.75rem`) and increase letter-spacing if keeping uppercase.
  - **Suggested command**: `typeset`
